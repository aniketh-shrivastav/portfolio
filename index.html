Project

AutoCustomizer

AutoCustomizer is a full-stack web application designed to help users customize their 4-wheeler vehicles. Unlike traditional repair platforms, this system focuses on vehicle customization — allowing users to select parts, book services, and manage their automotive preferences in one place.

The platform includes four types of users:

  • Admin – Oversees the platform, manages users, approves products, monitors orders and bookings, and generates commission reports.
  • Seller – Uploads car parts (with image, brand, price, and compatibility) for approval. Manages products and views earnings.
  • Service Provider – Offers customization services based on district. Manages bookings and tracks earnings via a dashboard.
  • Customer – Browses parts, adds to cart, places orders, books services by district/type, views history, and updates their profile.

Key features include secure login/signup with hashed passwords, role-based access, cart and booking system, booking/order history, profile management, product approval system, and commission tracking.
